Frequently Asked Questions

Click the "Add Bot" button in the navigation bar or use the invite link. Make sure you have "Manage Server" permissions to add bots to your Discord server.

Join a voice channel and use the +play [song name] command. You can search by song name, paste a YouTube/Spotify URL, or use playlist links.

Make sure the bot has proper permissions (Connect and Speak) in your voice channel. Also verify that you're in a voice channel when using the command. Try using +join manually.

Use the +prefix [new prefix] command. You need Administrator or Manage Server permissions to change the prefix. Example: +prefix !

You can get premium by voting for the bot on Top.gg (temporary premium) or by purchasing a premium subscription. Use +premium to check your status and +buy to see purchase options.
